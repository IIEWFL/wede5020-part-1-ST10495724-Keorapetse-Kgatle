About Bright Nation

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Bright Nation began as a small community project in Gauteng. Over time, we've grown into a registered non-profit organisation partnering with local councils and allies.Our mission is to uplift domestic workers and gardeners,while our vision is a South Africa where they feel valued and seen.
